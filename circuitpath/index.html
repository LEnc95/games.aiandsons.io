Circuit Path

Collect every cyan data node, dodge red sentry sweeps, then reach the uplink tile before the timer expires.

Desktop: Arrow keys or WASD move one tile, P pause, R restart, F fullscreen.

Mobile: tap directional buttons to step tiles. Red sweeps cost integrity, so route carefully.
