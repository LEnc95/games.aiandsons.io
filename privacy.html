Privacy Policy

Effective date: March 6, 2026

Cade's Games is designed to be school and kid friendly. By default, gameplay data is stored on the device in local browser storage.

What We Store

  • Profile name (if entered)
  • Coins, badges, inventory, cosmetics, recent games
  • Classroom mode settings (if enabled)
  • Per-game local high scores and settings

What We Do Not Collect By Default

  • No account is required to play
  • No chat, social feed, or direct messaging
  • No behavioral ad profiling
  • No third-party analytics trackers in this site build

How Data Is Used

  • To save progress and unlocks between sessions
  • To enforce classroom controls when enabled
  • To render local game history and achievements

Data Sharing

Gameplay data in this build is local-first and not sent to a remote data broker. If hosted services are added later, this page must be updated before release.

Parent and School Controls

  • Clear browser storage to remove local data
  • Use classroom mode to whitelist games and lock shop actions during sessions
  • Review school-specific commitments on the School Data page

Policy Updates

Material changes to data collection or monetization require a policy update and a new effective date.
